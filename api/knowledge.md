## The Venue — The Margi Farm

- The Margi Farm is the countryside estate of **The Margi Hotel** on the Athens Riviera.
- A **ten-acre (10-acre) organic farm** of olive groves, vineyards, and gardens, located in **Kalyvia, Attica**, just minutes from Athens International Airport.
- The farm was created in **2014** by the **Agiostratitis family** (Yiagos & Theo) to supply their home, hotel, and restaurants with organic produce, using traditional, sustainable farming methods.
- Alongside the gardens, olive groves, and vineyards, the farm has chickens, goats, horses, and a rescue donkey named Billy. It has 100-year-old olive trees and vineyards used to make homemade Tsipouro.
- **Dining:** Menus are crafted by Michelin-starred chef **Panagiotis Giakalis**, served farm-to-table with produce grown on the farm.
- **Website:** https://margifarm.gr/
- **Map location (coordinates):** 37.84874, 23.912944 (Kalyvia / Thorikou, East Attica)

## Travel — Getting There

- Fly into **Athens International Airport "Eleftherios Venizelos" (ATH)** — Greece's main hub, with direct flights from across Europe, the Middle East, and North America.
- The Margi Farm is just **~15 minutes** from the airport.

## Travel — Getting Around

- **Uber** works throughout Athens (it dispatches licensed local taxis), and pre-booked private transfers are easy to arrange in advance.
- Renting a car is simple — the farm has parking.
- It's a short drive to the Athens Riviera, and roughly **25–30 minutes** to central Athens.

## Travel — Entry, Visa & Money

- Greece is part of the **EU and the Schengen Area**. Most visitors (US, UK, EU, and many others) need **no visa for short stays**.
- The currency is the **Euro (€)**.
- Note: the EU's new **ETIAS** travel authorisation is expected in late 2026 — guests should check official EU sources closer to their trip.

## Travel — Weather

- Early September in Athens is **warm and dry** — around **28–32°C (82–90°F)** by day, with balmy evenings.
- Perfect for an outdoor celebration; pack light, breathable fabrics and a layer for later in the night.

## Things to Do in Athens & Greece

The couple is finalising detailed recommendations on the website's "Things to Do" page. High-level highlights nearby:

- **Ancient Athens:** The Acropolis & Parthenon, the Acropolis Museum, and the Ancient Agora — the heart of the classical city.
- **Old Town & Markets:** Plaka and Monastiraki — winding lanes, tavernas, rooftop views of the Acropolis, and the flea market.
- **Athens Riviera:** Vouliagmeni, Glyfada, and Lake Vouliagmeni — beaches, beach clubs, and seaside dining minutes from the venue.
- **Cape Sounion:** The clifftop Temple of Poseidon at sunset, about 45 minutes south along the coast.
- **Islands & day trips:** The Saronic islands (Aegina, Hydra, Poros) by ferry, or trips to Delphi and Nafplio.
- **Food & drink:** Greek mezze, fresh seafood, and the farm-to-table cuisine of The Margi.
